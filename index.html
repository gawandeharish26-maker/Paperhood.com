The Process

From file to
your doorstep

A simple, streamlined process designed so you can focus on writing — we handle the rest.

01

Upload Your Book

Submit your manuscript as a PDF. We accept A4, A5, and custom sizes. Interior and cover uploads are separate so you stay flexible.

02

Choose Specifications

Select paper type, binding style (paperback or hardcover), quantity, and any finishing options like matte or gloss lamination.

03

We Print & Deliver

Our facility prints your order within 48 hours and ships directly to your address with full tracking provided via email and SMS.

04

Hold Your Book

Receive your professionally printed book and share it with the world. Reorder anytime — your specs are saved for convenience.
